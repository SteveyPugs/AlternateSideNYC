What is Alternate Side NYC?
Alternate Side NYC is a web application that provides a live feed to New York City Alternate Side Parking Rules.

Features provided by Alternate Side NYC:
  • The Current Date and Alternate Side Parking Status
  • 10 Day Outlook Status along w/ weather:
    • Daily High Tempature
    • Daily Low Tempature
    • Daily Condition
  • Future Dates that NYC Alternate Side Parking will be suspended
  • Current NYC Alternate Side Parking Signs at your location (available in 5 boros only)
  • SMS Texting regarding the Alternate Side Parking Status (Free)
Alternate Side NYC is designed for mobile devices (Tablets Coming Soon).
Why design it?
A few years ago I moved to Queens. I love it here, but Alternate Side Parking is a major hassle. When you have to move your car at least once a week and everyone is parking on the same side it becomes rather dificult. If you decide to stick it to the man (like I have attempted numerous times and failed) you get a $45 parking ticket! When I first moved in I decided I needed an app that would help me figure some of this out. I found a few, one I used for a long time but there was lots of room for improvement. That's when I decided that I was going to develop something myself. I wanted to go above and beyond what was already out there.
